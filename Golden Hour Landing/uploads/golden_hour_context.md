# Golden Hour AI — Core Business Context

## What We Are
Golden Hour AI is an AI Transformation Partner (AITP) for SMBs, founded by Simone, based in Siena, Tuscany, Italy.
We guide small and medium businesses through a structured 3-step journey to introduce AI into their operations — from awareness and education, through strategic consulting, to hands-on development and implementation.
We are not a generic software agency. We are a transformation partner: we walk alongside our clients from "we don't know where to start" to "AI is working inside our business."

## Our 3-Step Methodology

### 1. Education
We run workshops inside the client's company — teaching the leadership team and staff what AI actually is, what benefits it can bring to their specific industry, and showing practical use cases they can relate to immediately.
Goal: remove fear and skepticism, build internal buy-in before any investment is made.

### 2. Consulting
We run a structured audit of the client's internal processes and bottlenecks. We then deliver a written report outlining which AI solutions are relevant to their business, what implementing each would require, and what measurable benefit they would gain.
Goal: give the client a clear, prioritized roadmap — not a generic pitch.

### 3. Development
We build the solutions identified in the consulting phase. This ranges from simple automations to custom AI tools tailored to the client's workflows and systems.
Goal: deliver working AI that actually gets used, not shelf ware.

## Who We Serve
SMBs across industries — we are not vertically locked to one sector.
Current active industries: hospitality, facility services/cleaning companies.
We work with any SMB that has operational bottlenecks, repetitive manual processes, or customer communication challenges that AI can meaningfully address.

## Products Portfolio

### AI Concierge (Hospitality)
Our first developed product. An AI agent that handles guest communication for hospitality businesses:
- Pre-booking inquiries (availability, pricing, house rules, local tips)
- During-stay support (check-in instructions, FAQs, local recommendations)
- Post-stay follow-up and review prompting
- Integration with Property Management Systems (PMS) where available via Duccio (Tobu) / Xenion

Target clients: B&Bs, agriturismos, short-term rental operators, property managers.

### Shift Scheduling Tool (Facility Services)
Currently in development for Simus Srl, a cleaning company.
An AI-powered tool to help manage and optimize employee shift scheduling during hospitality high season — where demand is variable and coordination is complex.
